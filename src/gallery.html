Our Campains and Checkup Teams.

Our campaigns and checkup teams are dedicated to promoting community health through various targeted initiatives:

  • Cardio CheckUp Camp: Provides essential heart health screenings and advice on preventing cardiovascular diseases.
  • Eye CheckUp Camp: Offers free eye exams, emphasizing the importance of vision care.
  • Covid Vaccination Unit: Instrumental in safeguarding the community against COVID-19 by administering vaccines and offering vital information.
  • Polio and PHC Unit: Focuses on polio vaccinations and primary healthcare, ensuring basic health services are accessible to all.

Through these efforts, we strive to enhance overall community wellbeing.Checkout our Some of the camp events here.
